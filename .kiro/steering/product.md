# Product Overview

Angelina AI is a WhatsApp-based AI agent for restaurant management that automates ordering, inventory management, complaint handling, and delivery coordination.

## Core Features

- **Automated Ordering**: Takes customer orders via WhatsApp with intelligent menu presentation
- **Smart Inventory Management**: Real-time stock monitoring and automatic updates
- **Complaint Handling**: Intelligent complaint analysis with automatic resolution suggestions
- **Delivery Coordination**: Optimized delivery scheduling based on location and urgency

## Web Application

The web app provides a SaaS dashboard for restaurant owners to:
- Manage company profile and team members
- Configure products/menu items with pricing and inventory
- Monitor subscription plans and usage metrics
- Track payment history and upgrade plans
- Connect WhatsApp business accounts

## Target Users

- Restaurant owners and managers
- Small to medium-sized food service businesses
- Multi-location restaurant chains (Enterprise plan)

## Business Model

Subscription-based SaaS with three tiers:
- **Starter**: $29/month - Small businesses (up to 5 team members)
- **Professional**: $79/month - Growing businesses (up to 25 team members)
- **Enterprise**: $199/month - Large organizations (unlimited team members)

## Localization

Supports French (default) and English with full i18n implementation.
